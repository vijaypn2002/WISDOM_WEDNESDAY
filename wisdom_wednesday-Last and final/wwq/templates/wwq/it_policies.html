IT Policies

At Lakshya, the IT department plays a crucial role in maintaining the technological infrastructure that supports our company's operations and growth. Our IT policies are designed to ensure that all employees use technology resources responsibly, securely, and effectively. These policies cover various aspects, including data protection, internet usage, software installation, and cybersecurity measures.

Every employee at Lakshya is expected to adhere to these IT policies to protect sensitive company information, maintain the integrity of our systems, and support a productive work environment. Compliance with these policies is essential for safeguarding our digital assets and ensuring that our technological tools are used in a way that aligns with our company values and objectives. The IT department is always available to assist with any technology-related issues or to clarify any aspects of these policies. Together, we can ensure a secure and efficient technological environment at Lakshya.
