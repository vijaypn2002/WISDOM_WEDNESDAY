ACCA (Association of Chartered Certified Accountants) - Details

All About ACCA

ACCA is a respected qualification granted by a globally known organisation. The full form of ACCA is Association of Chartered Certified Accountants. It is based in the UK, with influence in over 180 countries. ACCA certification is ideal for aspirants looking for a global career in accounting or finance. Choosing ACCA opens doors to advancing your career and achieving the coveted status of a chartered certified accountant within two to three years.

You can start preparing for ACCA early, soon after the 12th Grade, and build a successful career at an early age. ACCA is an inclusive program which invites young aspirants from diverse streams to learn ACCA and earn the opportunity to expand their career horizons worldwide. The qualification comprises three progressive levels: Applied Knowledge, Applied Skills, and Strategic Professional, each level having a set of exams all tied together with hands-on practical experience expectations to attain the professional qualification. This program goes the extra mile by providing flexible study choices, such as full-time, part-time, and online, to meet the diverse needs of its global student community. Moreover, the flexibility extends to exam attempts, as there's no group system hindrance.

Sub Courses:

  • ACCA Knowledge Level (KL): Business & Technology, Management Accounting, Financial Accounting, Global Law
  • ACCA Skills Level (SL): Performance Management, Tax, Financial Reporting, Audit and Assurance, Financial Management
  • ACCA Professional Level (PL): Strategic Business Leader, Strategic Business Reporting, Advanced Financial Management, Advanced Performance Management, Advanced Tax, Advanced Audit and Assurance

CA (Chartered Accountancy) - Details

All About CA

The CA course stands out as the supreme choice of aspiring students worldwide; numerous students and working professionals enroll for the course each year to become Chartered Accountants. The Chartered Accountancy (CA) program in India is governed by the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI).

Chartered Accountants have numerous esteemed job prospects in India and abroad, offering attractive salary packages. As a CA, you have the flexibility to either establish your independent practice or secure positions in the financial departments of various firms and companies.

The CA course comprises three levels: Foundation, Intermediate, and Final. The Foundation level is for those entering the course after 12th grade, while graduates can directly enter the Intermediate level. The Final level includes advanced topics in accountancy, law, and finance, along with mandatory practical training.

Sub Courses:

  • CA Foundation: Accountancy, Business Law, Logical Reasoning, Statistics, Business Correspondence and Reporting (BCR), Economics, Business and Commercial Knowledge (BCK), Mathematics
  • CA Intermediate: Accounts, Indirect Tax, Direct Tax, Law, Costing, Advanced Accounts, Auditing, Enterprise Information System, Strategic Management, Economics for Finance, Financial Management
  • CA Final: Law, Strategic Financial Management (SFM), Financial Reporting (FR), Auditing, Strategic Cost Management and Performance Evaluation (SCMPE), Direct Tax (DT), Indirect Tax (IDT)

CMA USA (Certified Management Accountant - USA) - Details

All About CMA USA

The Certified Management Accountant (CMA USA) is the gold standard in global management accounting provided by the Institute for Management Accountants (IMA), USA. The prestigious qualification is recognized in more than 150 countries, making the CMA USA Course a big deal worldwide.

The CMA USA curriculum covers two parts: Part 1 focuses on Financial Reporting, Planning, Performance, and Control, while Part 2 covers Financial Decision Making. The course is designed for professionals seeking to enhance their skills in financial management and strategic decision-making.

The CMA USA certification can be completed within 6-12 months, depending on your pace of study. It is ideal for those looking to advance their careers in financial management roles, such as Financial Analyst, Risk Manager, or CFO.

Sub Courses:

  • CMA Part 1: Financial Reporting, Planning Budgeting and Forecasting, Performance Management, Cost Management, Internal Controls, Technology and Analytics
  • CMA Part 2: Financial Statement Analysis, Corporate Finance, Decision Analysis, Risk Management, Investment Decisions, Professional Ethics

CMA India (Certified Management Accountant - India) - Details

All About CMA India

The CMA India is a prestigious qualification offered by the Institute of Cost Accountants of India (ICMAI); the course enables candidates to build a career as a skilled cost and management accountant.

The CMA India course is divided into three levels: Foundation, Intermediate, and Final. Each level covers topics like cost accounting, financial management, taxation, and business laws, providing a comprehensive education in management accounting.

CMA India certification is valuable for those looking to work in cost management, budgeting, and financial analysis in Indian industries. The course duration typically ranges from 2-3 years, depending on the student's pace of study.

Sub Courses:

  • CMA Foundation: Fundamentals of Management, Fundamentals of Accounting, Fundamentals of Laws and Ethics, Fundamentals of Business Mathematics, Statistics
  • CMA Intermediate: Financial Accounting, Business Laws and Ethics, Direct Taxation, Indirect Taxation, Cost Accounting, Operations Management, Strategic Management, Corporate Accounting, Financial Management, Management Accounting, Business Data Analytics, Audit

CS (Company Secretary) - Details

All About CS

CS is a professional degree provided by the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI); the company secretary plays a pivotal role in a firm, directly managing the company's strategy and decision-making processes and ensuring that all operations align with legal, moral, and regulatory standards.

The CS course is divided into three stages: Foundation, Executive, and Professional. The Foundation course is for those entering after 12th grade, while graduates can start with the Executive program. The Professional program is the final stage, focusing on advanced legal and regulatory aspects of corporate management.

CS professionals are in demand for their expertise in corporate governance, legal compliance, and advisory roles in various companies. The course duration is around 3 years, including mandatory practical training.

Sub Courses:

  • CS Executive Entrance Test (EET): Business Communication, Logical Reasoning, Legal Aptitude, Economics, Business Environment, Current Affairs, Communication Skills
  • CS Executive: Jurisprudence, Interpretation, General Laws, Company Law & Practice, Setting up of Business Entities and Closure, Corporate Accounting, Financial Management, Tax Laws (Indirect Tax & Direct Tax), Securities Laws & Capital Markets, Economic, Business, and Commercial Laws

ACCA + Integrated UG PG Degree - Details

ACCA Integrated Learning with UG/PG

The ACCA + Integrated UG PG Degree program is designed to provide a competitive edge to students by combining the globally recognized ACCA qualification with an undergraduate and postgraduate degree.

This unique program allows students to pursue an ACCA qualification alongside their UG/PG studies, providing exemptions for several ACCA exams. The integrated approach ensures a comprehensive education in accounting, finance, and management, making graduates highly competitive in the global job market.

The program typically takes 4-5 years to complete, depending on the chosen UG/PG degree and the student's pace of study. It is ideal for those looking to fast-track their careers in accounting and finance.
