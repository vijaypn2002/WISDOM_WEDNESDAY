Lakshya Commerce LMS

Minimum System Requirements

To ensure optimal user experience, please follow the minimum system requirements:

  • Browser: Latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, or Opera.
  • PC: Windows 8.1 or higher with 4GB RAM or more.
  • Mac: Mac OS X 10.12 or higher with 4GB RAM or more.
  • Mobile: iOS 13.0 and above, Android 8.0 and above.
  • Network: Fiber network with minimum 8Mbps download speed.

Getting Started as a Participant

Follow these steps to start using the Lakshya Commerce LMS:

  • Receive an email with a link to set your password.
  • Login using your email and password.
  • Reset your password if needed by clicking the 'Reset your password' link.
  • Access the LMS home page after logging in.
  • Update your profile and password in the 'My Profile' section.

Inside a Course Module

Steps to access and manage your course modules:

  • View your assigned courses and click 'Launch Course' to access them.
  • Complete assessments and assignments as directed within the modules.
  • Track your progress and view completion status of each module.

Attending Your Live Classes

Ways to attend live classes and view their schedule:

  • Access the 'Live Class and Recordings' section within the course modules.
  • Use the Calendar to view upcoming sessions and join them directly.
  • Use the Dashboard to join today’s session from the 'Join' button.

Learning through Recorded Classes

Methods to view recordings of live classes:

  • View recordings from the Calendar by selecting the date of the live session.
  • Access recordings from the 'Live Class and Recordings' section in the course module.
  • Use the 'My Progress' window in the Dashboard to view and manage your recordings.

Features to Keep You Up to Date

  • Receive notifications and announcements in real-time under the 'Notifications' and 'Announcements' sections.
  • Use the Discussion Forum to engage with other participants and facilitators.

Open a Support Ticket

If you encounter issues, use the 'Support' option to create a new ticket and access the Knowledge Base for common questions and solutions.
